WAAQOLOMA

OGOW QABIILKIISA / QABIILKEEDA

Ku raadi magaca buuxa ee qof caan ah ama qof taariikhi ah.

Af-Soomaali: WAAQOLOMA waa mareeg tixraac dadweyne oo loogu talagalay dadka caanka ah iyo shakhsiyaadka taariikhiga ah. Macluumaadku wuxuu ku salaysan yahay ilo dadweyne, wareysiyo, qoraallo taariikheed iyo caddeymo la soo gudbiyey. Xogta qabiilku way kala duwanaan kartaa, way dhammaystirnaan weydo ama sixid bay u baahan kartaa. Haddii aad khalad aragto, fadlan codsi sixid noo soo dir.

English: WAAQOLOMA is a public reference site for notable and historical figures. Information is based on public sources, interviews, historical records and submitted evidence. Clan affiliations may be disputed, incomplete or subject to correction. Please submit a correction request if you believe information is inaccurate.

RUNTA RAADSO, XOGTA XAQIIJI.
